---
title: "LineStrips3D"
---

A batch of line strips with positions and optional colors, radii, labels, etc.

## Components

**Required**: `LineStrip3D`

**Recommended**: `Radius`, `Color`

**Optional**: `Text`, `ClassId`, `InstanceKey`
