## What is Rerun Dataplatform?

Rerun Dataplatform extends Rerun's capabilities from local development to production-scale data infrastructure. It provides:

- **Managed storage**: Store and organize your recordings in the cloud
- **Scalable queries**: Query large datasets efficiently
- **Collaboration**: Share data and visualizations with your team
- **Integrations**: Connect with your existing ML and data infrastructure
